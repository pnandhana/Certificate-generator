How Piranha works!

Piranha is a tool used for creating multiple certificates at ease. User has to provide spreadsheet and certificate template. The uploaded spreadsheet and template is integrated using our special tool and certificates are generated corresponding to the count in spreadsheet. User can download the generated items to their device and voila! you finished that big job in seconds.
